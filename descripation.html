DESCRIPTION

This nuzzly, alluring fragrance captures the magic of warm sunset evening in the South of France. Infused with lively notes of Tangerine, sensual patchouli, rich amber and creamy sandalwood, Ambre Maquis is a warm, complex yet irresistible blend.

Amber
